🍽️ AR Smart Menu - Sample Marker

Print this marker and place it on your menu or table

📋 How to Use This Marker

  1. Print this page on white paper (A4 or Letter size)
  2. Compile the marker at MindAR Compiler
  3. Upload the printed marker image to the compiler
  4. Download the generated .mind file
  5. Save it as targets/targets.mind in your project
  6. Place the printed marker on your menu or table
  7. Scan with the AR Smart Menu app!
